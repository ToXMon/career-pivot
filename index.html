Common Questions

How does SMS coaching work? +
You text your coach whenever you have a thought, win, or frustration. Our AI analyzes your messages to identify patterns, themes, and blockers. Based on what we learn about your situation, we generate micro-sized tasks (15/30/60 min) you can accomplish with your current constraints. Weekly, we send you updates on themes and a prioritized roadmap.
What if I don't know what I want to do next? +
That's exactly what CareerPivot solves. Most people don't have a clarity problem‚Äîthey have an exploration problem. We start by asking you to vent about what you *don't* want. From those patterns, we build exploration tasks: 30 min research sprints, 1-hour informational interviews, mini skill experiments. Clarity emerges through action, not overthinking.
Is this a therapist or career coach? +
Neither and both. We're not licensed therapists (if you need mental health support, please reach out to a professional). We're not traditional career coaches (no 1-hour sessions, no $300/hr). We're accountability software with AI coaching. Think of us as your daily accountability partner who turns venting into action.
What if I can't afford to take a pay cut? +
You don't have to. Many CareerPivot users are finding lateral moves (same pay, different company/role) or slow transitions. The constraint is a feature, not a bug. It forces creativity. We plan around your financial needs, not against them. Some users are building side projects that eventually become their main income.
How much time do I need daily? +
As little as 15 minutes. You text a quick vent or win (2-3 min), and you get 1-3 tasks. One might be 15 min of research, another a 30-min informational interview, another a micro-skill experiment. No 10+ hour weekly commitment. Fits into real life.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No lock-in, no penalties. Founding members get the locked-in $19/month price forever if you stay, but there's zero obligation. Text STOP to opt out of SMS, or reply "cancel" and we'll handle the rest within 48 hours.
Is my data private? +
Yes. We don't sell data. We don't share SMS content with third parties. We use your messages to improve your coaching experience and aggregate insights for product development (anonymized). Full privacy policy here.
What happens after the founding member period? +
Your $19/month price locks in forever. As we add features (premium integrations, group coaching, career community), you'll have the option to upgrade, but your base price stays locked. Founding members get first access to new features.
